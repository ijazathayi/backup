Module 1: Financial Literacy

Chapter 1: Money and Wealth Creation

Money is a medium of exchange that allows goods and services to be traded efficiently. In modern economies money also acts as a store of value and unit of account.

Functions of Money

  • Medium of Exchange
  • Store of Value
  • Unit of Account
  • Deferred Payment Standard
+-------------------+ | MONEY | +-------------------+ | +-----+-----+ | | Exchange Savings | | Spending Investing
Investing is the process of making money work for you instead of simply saving it in cash.
Chapter 2: Inflation

Inflation is the rate at which prices rise over time. As inflation increases, purchasing power decreases.

Inflation Effect
Low Stable economy
Moderate Growth phase
High Reduced purchasing power
