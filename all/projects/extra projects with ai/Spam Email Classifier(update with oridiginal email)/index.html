How SpamShield Works

SpamShield uses a multi-signal classification engine combining heuristic rules, keyword analysis, sender reputation patterns, and structural analysis to deliver accurate results.

Keyword Detection

Identifies 200+ known spam trigger words and phrases used in phishing and fraudulent emails.

Sender Analysis

Analyses email domains, suspicious TLDs, and sender patterns for anomaly detection.

Structure Analysis

Evaluates formatting patterns — excessive capitals, punctuation, urgency language, and link density.

Context Scoring

Multi-factor confidence scoring provides probability score with signal-by-signal breakdown.
